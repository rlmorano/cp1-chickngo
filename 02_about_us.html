About Chick 'En Go

History

Originally known as Vergara’s Chicken from 1987 on and initially located at Vergara Supermarket in Dinalupihan, the business of selling roasted chicken flourished until a fire destroyed the supermarket. Chick ‘En Go was the revived brand by Genesis Vergara, son of the original owner and reopened re branded at Vergara Supermarket 2016. Within a year Chick ‘En Go grew to a multi-outlet business and volume of chicken sold increased rapidly. Quality and affordable roasted chicken and pork liempo are served in multiple outlets now with exceptional customer service.
Mission

To continuously provide superior level of service and quality of best tasting roasted chicken and liempo at a price appealing to every customer.

Vision

To continuously innovate products and business models for our franchisees.
